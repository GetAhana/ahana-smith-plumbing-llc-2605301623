What We Do

The services that drive the most calls
in Phoenix

Smith Plumbing provides residential plumbing repair, water heater service, and drain clearing to homeowners throughout Phoenix and the surrounding valley. Every service comes with upfront pricing, professional workmanship, and our one-year labor warranty.

Repairs

From dripping faucets to whole-bathroom repipes, we track down the leak, show you repair-or-replace options with upfront pricing, and finish with pressure tests and cleanup so your water stays where it belongs.

Water Heaters

We size tank and tankless units for your home, handle safe venting and connections, flush or swap failing heaters, haul away the old unit, and walk you through warranty coverage before we leave.

Drains

Kitchen, bath, and main-line backups get cabled or hydro-jetted with camera follow-up when needed, clear written findings, and coaching on what caused the slow drain so it is less likely to return.
