Our Story

Why it
matters

We started Smith Plumbing because we believe homeowners deserve straightforward, fair-priced plumbing work without the runaround. Most of our growth has come through word of mouth—customers who trust us enough to recommend us to friends and neighbors. That trust is everything.

Plumbing problems are stressful. Water damage, backed-up drains, and failed water heaters can disrupt your home and your schedule. We listen carefully, diagnose quickly, and explain your options in clear language. Whether it's a dripping faucet or a whole-home repipe, we treat every job with the same level of care.

Our team brings 14+ years of on-site experience and a deep commitment to doing the job right the first time. We show up when we say we will, deliver transparent pricing upfront, and finish with pressure tests and cleanup. That's the Smith Plumbing promise.
